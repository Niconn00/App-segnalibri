The Shadow Realms

By Anya Petrova

Fantasy | 2023

Synopsis

In a world where shadows hold secrets and magic weaves through the very fabric of existence, Elara, a young sorceress, embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a looming darkness threatening to consume her realm. With ancient prophecies as her guide and newfound allies by her side, Elara must confront her own inner demons and unravel the mysteries of the Shadow Realms before it's too late.
